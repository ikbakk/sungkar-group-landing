## Outreach Strategy Notes

- **Discover Lombok** is the fastest win: self-serve listing, free tier available, immediate presence in Lombok tourism directory alongside 121 other tour agencies.
- **10Adventures** and **Power Traveller** are the strongest editorial inclusions: both have explicit partnership programs for tour operators.
- **Travel blogs** (Explore Trips Guide, We Are Global Travellers, The Gone Goat) should be approached with personalized reference to their specific Lombok article content — generic pitches won't work.
- **Viator/GetYourGuide-based sites** (Things To Do In Lombok, Happy To Visit) are harder to influence directly since they aggregate from those platforms. Getting listed on Viator/GetYourGuide first would open these doors.
- **Indonesian directories** (Pdberger, Igresikgo360) require Indonesian-language outreach and are likely lower traffic but easier to get listed on.
- **Direct competitors** (Green Rinjani, The Langkah Travel, Lombok Travelutions, etc.) were identified but deprioritized — outreach may be framed as cross-referral or tourism ecosystem collaboration rather than link-building.
