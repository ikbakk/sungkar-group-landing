# Preparation Before Your Lombok Vacation

An enjoyable vacation starts with proper preparation. Here are things you need to prepare before heading to Lombok.

## 1. Travel Documents

Make sure your ID/passport and flight tickets are ready. For foreign tourists, ensure your visa is in order.

## 2. Accommodation

Book your hotel or lodging well in advance, especially during holiday season.

## 3. Transportation

Decide on transportation during your stay: car rental, private driver, or a combination.

## 4. Packing

Bring light clothing, sunscreen, hat, and personal medication.

## 5. Budget

Prepare enough cash as not all places accept credit cards.

## 6. Itinerary

Create a simple itinerary for a more organized and efficient vacation.
