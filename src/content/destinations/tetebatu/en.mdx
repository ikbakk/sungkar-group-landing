description: >
  Tetebatu is a mountain village on the southern slopes of Mount Rinjani offering an authentic Lombok rural experience. Far from the coastal crowds, Tetebatu delights visitors with green terraced rice paddies, coffee and cacao gardens, and cool mountain air.

  Light trekking around Tetebatu takes you through rice fields, small forests, and local plantations. The trails to Jukut and Jeruk Manis Waterfalls are fairly accessible with a local guide, suitable for all fitness levels. Along the way, you'll see farmers working in the paddies and friendly village children.

  Tetebatu is also an appealing alternative for those who want to experience the Rinjani atmosphere without climbing. The view of Mount Rinjani from the Tetebatu Monkey Forest area is stunning in the morning. The peaceful village atmosphere, friendly residents, and slow pace of life make Tetebatu a favorite for slow travel and cultural tourism.
