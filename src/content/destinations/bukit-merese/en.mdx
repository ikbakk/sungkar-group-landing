description: >
  Merese Hill is a panoramic hill overlooking Tanjung Aan Beach in the Mandalika area. From its peak — just 15–20 minutes of light walking — you are treated to a spectacular 360-degree view: the curved Tanjung Aan bay, Mandalika's long coastline, and the deep blue ocean stretching to Bali in the distance.

  This hill is the most popular sunset and sunrise spot in South Lombok. In the late afternoon, visitors gather at the peak to watch the sun dip behind the hills with skies shifting from orange to purple. The green grasslands covering the hill create a beautiful contrast with the blue sea.

  Trekking at Merese Hill is very light and suitable for all ages. The trail is a clear dirt path with a gentle incline. Around the peak there are several natural photo spots with different backdrops — cliffs, grasslands, and bay views.
