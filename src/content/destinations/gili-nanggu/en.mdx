description: >
  Gili Nanggu is a small tropical island in the Sekotong waters of West Lombok. Unlike the three main Gilis to the north, Gili Nanggu offers a more private and exclusive experience — ideal for travelers wanting to enjoy underwater beauty without the crowds.

  The waters around Gili Nanggu are known for shallow snorkeling that's safe for all ages. Healthy coral reefs and a variety of tropical fish can be enjoyed just meters from the shoreline. The soft white sand and calm waters make this island a favorite for families with young children and older travelers.

  Island hopping packages from Sekotong typically include Gili Nanggu, Gili Sudak, and Gili Kedis in one day — a perfect combination of snorkeling, seafood lunch, and exploring tiny exotic islands.
