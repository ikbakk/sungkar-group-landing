description: >
  Gili Sudak is one of the small islands in the Sekotong area of West Lombok, part of the popular island hopping route alongside Gili Nanggu and Gili Kedis. The island is known for its beachfront seafood restaurant serving fresh fish and seafood against a backdrop of white sand and clear water.

  Unlike Gili Nanggu which focuses more on snorkeling, Gili Sudak is the main rest stop in the island hopping route. After snorkeling in the waters around Nanggu and Kedis, visitors usually stop here for lunch with signature dishes of grilled fish, fried squid, and Lombok-style sambal.

  Gili Sudak's beach is relatively calm with small waves, perfect for relaxing while enjoying a young coconut. The island also has shallow snorkeling areas around the jetty, although the main spots remain at Gili Nanggu and Gili Kedis.
