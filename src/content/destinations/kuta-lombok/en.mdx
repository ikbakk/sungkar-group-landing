description: >
  Kuta Lombok is a coastal area in South Lombok that has grown into a major tourist destination. Unlike the busy Kuta in Bali, Kuta Lombok offers a more laid-back atmosphere with a long coastline, green hills, and easy access to various exotic beaches nearby.

  Kuta Beach itself has unique white sand shaped like peppercorns — a texture different from most beaches. From here you can explore the iconic Tanjung Aan Beach, Seger Beach, Merese Hill for sunset, and Selong Belanak for learning to surf.

  The area is also the hub for accommodation and dining in South Lombok. Dozens of cafés, restaurants, and boutique hotels line the main road, offering everything from specialty coffee to traditional Sasak and international cuisine.
