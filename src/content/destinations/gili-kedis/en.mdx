description: >
  Gili Kedis is a tiny white-sand island in the Sekotong waters that has become a social media sensation for its exotic beauty. The island is very small — you can walk around it in less than 5 minutes — yet its charm is extraordinary.

  Gili Kedis's signature feature is the iconic wooden swing set up on the beachfront, creating a perfect photo spot with a backdrop of white sand, clear blue water, and tropical skies. The water around the island is very shallow and calm, ideal for wading or light snorkeling. Starfish are often visible on the clear sandy bottom.

  Due to its small size, Gili Kedis is usually just a short stop on the island hopping route. There are no accommodation or restaurant facilities here — just pure natural beauty. The island is best visited during moderate tide; at low tide the sand area is wider and better for walking around.
