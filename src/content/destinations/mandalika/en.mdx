description: >
  Mandalika is a Special Economic Zone (KEK) for tourism in South Lombok encompassing Kuta Beach, Tanjung Aan, Merese Hill, and the surrounding area. The zone is being developed as a world-class integrated tourism destination with modern infrastructure while preserving its natural beauty.

  Mandalika's newest icon is the Pertamina Mandalika International Street Circuit, which hosts MotoGP and World Superbike events. The circuit sits right by the beach with spectacular green hills as a backdrop — making it one of the most beautiful racing circuits in the world.

  Beyond racing events, Mandalika offers exotic beaches with white sand and clear waters. Tanjung Aan Beach with its dual sand textures, Merese Hill with its 360-degree panorama, and Seger Beach with the annual Bau Nyale tradition are the main attractions that can be enjoyed year-round.
