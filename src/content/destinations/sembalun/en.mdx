description: >
  Sembalun is a highland area in East Lombok situated at the foot of Mount Rinjani. At about 1,000m above sea level, Sembalun offers cool mountain air and a unique landscape — savanna hills that turn golden in the dry season and vibrant green during the rainy season.

  This area is the main entry point for Rinjani trekking via the eastern route. However, Sembalun has its own attractions beyond trekking. Pergasingan Hill offers a 360-degree panorama with views of Rinjani, the Sembalun valley, and the ocean in the distance — popular for short camping trips and sunrise hikes.

  Sembalun is also known as a horticultural center in Lombok. Strawberry farms, vegetable gardens, and Sembalun garlic are signature products you can buy directly from farmers. The cool climate and mountain scenery make it a perfect escape from the coastal heat.
