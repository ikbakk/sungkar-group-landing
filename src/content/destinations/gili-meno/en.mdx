description: >
  Gili Meno is the smallest and quietest of the three main Gili islands. Known as a honeymoon and romantic escape destination, the island offers long stretches of white-sand beaches, crystal-clear blue waters, and an exceptionally private atmosphere.

  Gili Meno's main draw is its serenity. Here you won't find parties or crowds — just the sound of waves, wind, and occasional footsteps on the sand. Popular snorkeling spots include the turtle point on the northwest side where green turtles are frequently sighted, and the "Nest" underwater sculpture by Jason deCaires Taylor — an iconic underwater photography subject.

  Accommodation on Gili Meno tends to be more limited and simple, but several boutique resorts offer exclusive beachfront stays. The island also has a small salt lake in its center that's unique to explore.
