description: >
  Gili Air is the second-smallest island in the Gili archipelago, offering the perfect balance between tranquility and tourist amenities. Not as busy as Gili Trawangan yet livelier than Gili Meno, this island is an ideal choice for travelers seeking a relaxed atmosphere without sacrificing comfort.

  The island is completely free of motor vehicles — the main modes of transport are walking, cycling, or cidomo (local horse carts). Gili Air's coastline is surrounded by clear waters with snorkeling spots accessible directly from the beach, especially on the east and north sides.

  The vibe on Gili Air is perfect for slow travel. Small cafés line the beachfront with bean bags and umbrellas, while in the evening seafood restaurants serve fresh catches under candlelight. Ideal for couples, backpackers, and families looking for a calmer holiday rhythm.
