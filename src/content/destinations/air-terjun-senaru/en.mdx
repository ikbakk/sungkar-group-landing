description: >
  Sendang Gile and Tiu Kelep are two spectacular waterfalls located in the Senaru area of North Lombok. Both are within the Mount Rinjani National Park and are a favorite destination for travelers wanting to enjoy natural beauty without summiting the peak.

  Sendang Gile is the first waterfall you encounter, about 10–15 minutes on foot from the entrance. It has a height of about 30 meters with a strong water flow, creating a refreshing mist around it. The trail to Sendang Gile is relatively easy with well-maintained stairs.

  Tiu Kelep is the star attraction. Located deeper — about 30–45 minutes trekking from Sendang Gile — this waterfall offers a wide natural pool you can swim in. The atmosphere at Tiu Kelep is magical: a large waterfall surrounded by dense tropical forest, echoing water sounds, and fine mist dancing on the pool's surface.
