description: >
  Pink Beach, or Pantai Tangsi, is a unique beach in East Lombok known for its pink-tinged sand. The distinctive color comes from fragments of red coral mixed with white sand — creating a beautiful gradient, especially under direct sunlight.

  There are three Pink Beach areas (Pink 1, Pink 2, Pink 3) in the Jerowaru region, each with different characteristics. Pink Beach 1 is the most popular with better facilities, while Pink 2 and 3 are quieter and more private. The waters are clear with shallow coral reefs ideal for snorkeling.

  Pink Beach Tour packages usually also visit Gili Pasir (a tidal sand island), Gili Petelu for snorkeling, and Tanjung Ringgit for dramatic sea-cliff panoramas. The overland journey from Kuta or Mataram takes quite a while but is well worth it for the scenery along the way.
