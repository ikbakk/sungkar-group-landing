description: >
  Senggigi is Lombok's first and most established tourist beach area. Located on the west coast, Senggigi offers a long coastline with direct sunset views towards Bali and Mount Agung — one of the best sunset panoramas in Lombok.

  As the island's oldest tourism hub, Senggigi has comprehensive tourist infrastructure. A string of hotels, resorts, restaurants, and bars line the coastal road, offering everything from budget stays to luxury boutique resorts. The beach itself has golden sand with relatively calm waves, suitable for casual swimming.

  Senggigi's location is very strategic — only about 45–75 minutes from Lombok Airport and close to Bangsal Harbor for access to the Gili Islands. Many travelers use Senggigi as a first-night or last-night basecamp before further Lombok exploration.
